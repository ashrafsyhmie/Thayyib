# Context Engineering

## Definition

Context engineering means giving AI the right information at the right time instead of dumping everything into one large prompt.

## Why It Matters

- Reduces token cost
- Improves accuracy
- Reduces hallucination
- Makes AI easier to debug

## Thayyib Context Layers

1. Company context
2. Supplier context
3. Document context
4. Ingredient knowledge
5. Audit checklist
6. Regulation notes
7. User role
