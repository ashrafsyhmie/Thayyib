# AI Guardrails

## Required Guardrails

- AI must not issue final religious/legal rulings
- AI must cite sources when possible
- AI must say uncertain when uncertain
- AI must recommend human review
- AI must not invent certification requirements
- AI must preserve data privacy

## User-Facing Disclaimer

Thayyib AI provides compliance assistance and risk detection. Final verification should be performed by qualified halal compliance personnel.
