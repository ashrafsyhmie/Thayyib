# FYP Proposal Draft

## Title

Thayyib: AI-Powered Halal Compliance Intelligence Platform for Food Manufacturers

## Problem

Halal compliance management is document-heavy and often handled manually by SMEs, leading to expired certificates, missing documents, and slow audit preparation.

## Objective

To develop a web-based AI-assisted platform that helps food manufacturers manage halal compliance documents, supplier certificates, and audit readiness.

## Scope

The system focuses on:
- Supplier tracking
- Document upload
- Certificate expiry monitoring
- AI document analysis
- Audit readiness dashboard

## Expected Outcome

A deployed SaaS prototype demonstrating practical AI document intelligence for halal compliance management.
