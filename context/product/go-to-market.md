# Go-To-Market Strategy

## First Customer Segment

Small halal food manufacturers in Malaysia.

## Outreach Channels

- LinkedIn
- SME food business groups
- Halal expo events
- University-industry networks
- Cold email to manufacturers
- Ask lecturers for industry contacts

## Validation Questions

- How do you currently manage halal documents?
- How do you track supplier certificates?
- Have you ever missed a renewal deadline?
- How long does audit preparation take?
- Would a dashboard and reminders help?
