# Vision

## Vision Statement

To become the intelligent compliance operating system for the halal industry.

## Mission

To help halal-certified manufacturers maintain trust, reduce compliance workload, and prepare for audits through smart software and AI-assisted analysis.

## Long-Term Expansion

After food manufacturing, Thayyib can expand to:
- Cosmetics
- Pharmaceuticals
- Logistics
- Warehousing
- Restaurant franchises
- Export readiness
- Food safety compliance

## Product Expansion Inside Food Manufacturing

Before expanding into other industries, Thayyib can grow deeper inside food manufacturers through Halal Operations Monitoring.

This future module can help branches, kitchens, and production teams monitor:

- Stock and ingredient expiry
- Batch-level compliance issues
- Branch-specific alerts
- Supplier or document warnings
- Kitchen kiosk visibility for urgent issues

This should remain compliance-focused and should not become a full ERP system in the early stage.
