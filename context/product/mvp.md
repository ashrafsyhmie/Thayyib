# MVP Definition

## MVP Goal

Enable a halal food manufacturer to know:

1. Are our documents organized?
2. Are our supplier certificates valid?
3. Are there risky ingredients or documents?
4. Are we ready for an audit?

## MVP Demo Flow

1. Login
2. View dashboard
3. Add supplier
4. Upload supplier certificate
5. Upload ingredient list
6. Run AI analyzer
7. View risk result
8. View audit readiness score
9. See expiring certificate reminder
