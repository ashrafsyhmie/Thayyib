# User Personas

## Compliance Officer

Responsible for ensuring halal documentation is complete and audit-ready.

Pain points:
- Too many documents
- Expired certificates
- Manual reminders
- Audit stress

## QA Manager

Oversees product quality and production standards.

Pain points:
- Ingredient uncertainty
- Supplier changes
- SOP tracking

## Factory Manager

Needs high-level visibility.

Pain points:
- No single compliance score
- Hard to know if audit-ready
- Operational disruption if certification issues occur

## External Auditor

May review evidence and status.

Pain points:
- Hard-to-navigate records
- Missing evidence
