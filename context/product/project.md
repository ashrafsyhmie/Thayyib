# Project Overview

## Name

Thayyib

## Category

B2B SaaS, RegTech, AI document intelligence, halal compliance software.

## One-Liner

Thayyib helps halal food manufacturers manage documents, suppliers, certificates, and audit readiness using AI-assisted compliance intelligence.

## Why It Matters

Halal compliance is a trust-based and regulation-sensitive process. Businesses need visibility, reminders, evidence, and audit preparation tools.

Thayyib helps reduce manual work and compliance risk.
